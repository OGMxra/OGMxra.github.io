Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 6, 2026

WAZAM is built on a simple principle: your audio is used to identify sounds, then discarded. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We collect only what is necessary to make the app work and improve over time.

1. Who We Are

WAZAM is an independent mobile application developed by Omar Guadalupe Mora Estrada, based in Hayward, California. WAZAM uses artificial intelligence to identify birds and other nature sounds in real time through your device's microphone.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@wazam.app

2. Information We Collect

Audio Recordings

When you tap the Listen button, WAZAM records audio through your device's microphone for up to 12 seconds. This audio is:

  • Transmitted securely to our servers for processing
  • Analyzed by our BirdNET-powered AI model to identify species
  • Deleted from our servers immediately after processing
  • Never stored permanently or associated with your identity

Location Data (Optional)

If you grant location permission, WAZAM uses your approximate geographic coordinates to filter species suggestions to birds and animals known to exist in your region. Location data is:

  • Used only to improve identification accuracy
  • Not stored permanently on our servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Entirely optional — the app functions without location access

Feedback Data

When you tap "Was this correct?" after an identification, we collect:

  • Whether the identification was correct or incorrect
  • The species that was identified
  • Your correction, if provided
  • The confidence level of the identification

This feedback is used exclusively to improve WAZAM's identification accuracy over time. It is not linked to your personal identity.

Usage Analytics

We collect basic anonymous usage data including app version, device type (iPhone/Android), and general region (country level). This helps us understand how the app is used and identify technical issues. No personally identifiable information is included.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, or account information (no account required)
  • Precise GPS coordinates stored long-term
  • Audio recordings stored after processing
  • Contacts, photos, or any data unrelated to sound identification
  • Device identifiers used for advertising
  • Browsing history or activity outside WAZAM

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Identify bird species and nature sounds from your audio recordings
  • Filter species suggestions based on your geographic region
  • Improve the accuracy of our AI identification models using feedback
  • Diagnose and fix technical problems with the app
  • Understand general usage patterns to improve the user experience

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. We share limited information only in these circumstances:

  • Service providers: We use Google Cloud to process audio and run our AI models. Google processes data on our behalf under strict data processing agreements and does not use your data for its own purposes.
  • eBird API (Cornell Lab): We send your general region code (e.g., "US-CA") to eBird's API to retrieve regional species lists. No personal information is transmitted.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

6. Data Retention

  • Audio recordings: Deleted immediately after processing (within seconds)
  • Location data: Used in real time, not retained
  • Feedback data: Retained indefinitely in anonymized form to train future models
  • Analytics: Retained for up to 12 months in aggregated, anonymous form

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Microphone access: You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings. The app will not function without it.
  • Location access: You can revoke location permission at any time. The app continues to work without location, with slightly reduced filtering accuracy.
  • Data deletion: Since we do not collect personally identifiable information, there is no personal profile to delete. Anonymized feedback data cannot be attributed to you and cannot be deleted individually.
  • Opt out of analytics: Contact us at privacy@wazam.app to request exclusion from analytics.

8. Children's Privacy

WAZAM is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information to us, please contact us at privacy@wazam.app and we will promptly delete it.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Audio files are processed in isolated server environments and deleted immediately
  • We use Google Cloud's security infrastructure, which meets SOC 2 and ISO 27001 standards
  • Access to our systems is restricted to authorized personnel only

10. Third-Party Services

WAZAM uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Cloud Run & Cloud Storage: Audio processing and model hosting. Google Cloud Privacy Policy
  • eBird API (Cornell Lab of Ornithology): Regional species data for location filtering. Cornell Lab Privacy Policy
  • BirdNET (Chemnitz University of Technology): Open-source AI model for species identification. No user data is transmitted to BirdNET servers — the model runs on our infrastructure.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as WAZAM grows and evolves. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of WAZAM after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
