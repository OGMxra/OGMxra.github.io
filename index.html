How it works

Point. Listen.
Know.

WAZAM's AI pipeline processes sound the way your brain processes vision — instantly and with context.

01
Tap Listen
WAZAM records 12 seconds of ambient audio through your phone's microphone.
02
Noise reduction
Wind, traffic, and background noise are filtered before analysis begins.
03
AI analysis
13 overlapping windows analyzed by BirdNET — Cornell's AI model trained on 6,522 species.
04
Local filtering
Results weighted for your region and time of day — no tropical birds suggested in California.
05
Instant result
Top 3 species displayed with confidence scores. Tap to learn more about each one.
06
You train the AI
Every Yes/No feedback tap teaches WAZAM to be more accurate for the next user.
Features

Built for the
real world.

Not a lab demo. WAZAM is designed for outdoor use — in wind, traffic noise, and bright sunlight.

🎙
Audio-first identification
No photo needed. Hear it → know it. WAZAM works before you even see the bird.
📍
Location-aware filtering
Uses your GPS to prioritize species actually known to exist in your region.
🌅
Time-of-day weighting
Dawn chorus? Nocturnal owls? WAZAM knows what's likely at every hour.
🔁
Self-improving AI
Every user correction trains the next model version. WAZAM gets smarter as it grows.
⚡
Instant results
Results in under 3 seconds on warm server. No waiting, no uploading, no account.
🔒
Privacy first
Audio processed and deleted immediately. No recordings stored. No data sold. Ever.
